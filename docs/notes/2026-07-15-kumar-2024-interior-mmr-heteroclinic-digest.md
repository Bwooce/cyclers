## Scope

The interior-resonance predecessor to the exterior-MMR paper above (AAS 25-569 cites this as its own
footnote 7). Computes and analyzes resonant periodic-orbit families in the planar Earth-Moon CR3BP,
focused on **interior 4:1, 3:1, and 2:1 resonances**:

- Identifies **bifurcations of the resonant periodic-orbit families**.
- Computes **stable/unstable manifolds** via an **osculating-orbit-perigee Poincaré map** (the same
  map-at-perigee construction used across this whole research thread — cf. the apogee-map variant
  used for exterior resonances).
- Characterizes the **range of naturally attainable semimajor-axis values** for future distant-Earth
  or lunar spacecraft, across a range of Jacobi-constant energies — i.e. mapping out which
  semimajor-axis transitions are dynamically "free" via heteroclinic connections between resonances.
- Framed explicitly around cislunar space-domain-awareness motivation (Artemis, Lunar Gateway,
  CLPS, Chang'e, Chandrayaan, SLIM — the same motivating context as the xGEO paper below).
