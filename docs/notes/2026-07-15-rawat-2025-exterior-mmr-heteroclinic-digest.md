## Scope

Direct sequel to the group's own prior interior-MMR work (footnote 7 in the abstract cites their own
earlier interior-resonance paper — i.e. `kumar-rawat-rosengren-ross-2024-IAC` below). This paper
extends the same Poincaré-map-at-apogee machinery to **exterior** 1:n resonances in the planar
Earth-Moon CR3BP (region beyond the Moon, within Earth's Hill sphere). Key findings per the abstract:

- Identifies **two stable asymmetric libration zones** and a **weak unstable symmetric resonance
  zone embedded within a strong unstable symmetric region** — a bifurcation signature from heightened
  instability, distinct from the interior-resonance case.
- The region of influence of each resonance is delineated by the **separatrices of unstable resonant
  periodic orbits** (same methodological backbone as the interior-MMR companion paper).
- Reports **heteroclinic connections with interior** resonances (abstract cuts off mid-sentence in the
  extracted text; full body not yet read).
